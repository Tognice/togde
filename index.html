What are my Research interests?

My research interests encompass a diverse range of fields, including Algebra, Cryptography and its Applications, Computer Security, and Artificial Intelligence. I am particularly fascinated by the mathematical foundations of cryptography, leveraging algebraic techniques to develop robust encryption methods. My work also explores the integration of AI with cryptographic systems to enhance security measures and protect sensitive information against emerging threats. By combining expertise in these areas, I aim to advance the field of computer security and contribute to the development of innovative solutions for safeguarding digital assets.
